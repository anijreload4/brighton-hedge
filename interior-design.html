Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our interior and exterior decoration services

What types of decoration services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of decoration services, including interior design, exterior decoration, home staging, and renovation services. Our team can help you create beautiful and functional spaces that reflect your style.

How long does a typical decoration project take?

The duration of a decoration project depends on various factors, including the size and complexity of the project. A small interior design project might take a few weeks, while a larger renovation could take several months. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide warranties for your decoration work?

Yes, we offer warranties for our decoration services. The specifics of the warranty depend on the type of work performed. We will discuss warranty details during the project planning phase.

Can you help with furniture selection and sourcing?

Absolutely! We provide furniture selection and sourcing services as part of our interior design offerings. Our team will help you choose pieces that fit your style and budget.

How do you ensure quality in your decoration projects?

Quality is paramount in our decoration projects. We ensure quality through careful planning, skilled craftsmanship, and regular quality checks throughout the project. Our team is dedicated to delivering exceptional results.
