Office Renovation Project

Project Overview

Health Systems Consult Limited approached us to renovate their office space, creating a modern, functional environment that reflects their corporate identity and enhances productivity.

Client:
Health Systems Consult Limited
Location:
Lagos, Nigeria
Project Type:
Office Renovation
Duration:
6 Weeks
Completion:
January 2023

Project Scope

  • Scraping of dilapidated walls/paint
  • Treatment of wall with chemicals to stop water retainment
  • Screeding and painting of office
  • Installation of window blinds, office furniture (tables, sofas, orthopedic chairs etc)

  • Industrial cleaning and polishing of toilet, tiles and office space
