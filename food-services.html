Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our food services

What types of food services do you offer?

We offer a variety of food services, including catering for events, food delivery, and event planning. Our team can customize services to meet your specific needs.

How do I place an order for catering?

You can place an order by contacting us through our website or by phone. We will discuss your requirements and provide a quote based on your needs.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary restrictions, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and more. Please inform us of any specific requirements when placing your order.

How far in advance do I need to book your services?

We recommend booking our services at least two weeks in advance to ensure availability and allow for proper planning. However, we will do our best to accommodate last-minute requests.

Can you provide references from past clients?

Yes, we can provide references from past clients upon request. We take pride in our work and are happy to share testimonials from satisfied customers.
