Our Story

Brighton Hedge Limited is a company in Nigeria with the aim of providing qualitative services to desiring clients. We offer top-notch Renovation and Remodelling services, Project Management, Interior and Exterior designs to residences, individuals, corporate, and commercial Organizations.

We provide a wide range of home and office solutions (Furnitures & Fittings) as well as engage in executing general contracts of all kinds. We also carry out cleaning services that rids your space of long lasting stain and dirt leaving your home sparkling clean with a breath of fresh air.

We are your no one go to plug for the above services as we provide qualitative services to clients and exceeds our customers expectations.

Who We Are

Brighton-Hedge Limited was established with a vision to transform spaces and exceed client expectations through innovative design solutions and quality craftsmanship. Our team consists of experienced professionals who are passionate about creating functional and aesthetically pleasing environments.

We understand that each client has unique needs and preferences, which is why we take a personalized approach to every project. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail have earned us a reputation as a trusted partner for renovation, remodeling, and interior design projects across Nigeria.

At Brighton-Hedge, we believe that successful projects are built on strong client relationships, clear communication, and a deep understanding of our clients' vision. We work closely with our clients throughout the entire process, from initial consultation to project completion, ensuring that every detail meets our high standards of quality.
